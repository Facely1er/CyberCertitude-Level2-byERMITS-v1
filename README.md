## üîß CMMC 2.0 Level 2 Coverage

### **CMMC Domains** (14 Total - CMMC 2.0)
1. **Access Control (AC)** - User access management and authentication
2. **Audit and Accountability (AU)** - System auditing and logging capabilities
3. **Awareness and Training (AT)** - Security awareness and training programs
4. **Configuration Management (CM)** - System configuration and change control
5. **Identification and Authentication (IA)** - User identification and authentication
6. **Incident Response (IR)** - Incident detection, response, and recovery
7. **Maintenance (MA)** - System maintenance and updates
8. **Media Protection (MP)** - Protection of system media and information
9. **Personnel Security (PS)** - Personnel screening and access management
10. **Physical Protection (PE)** - Physical access and environmental controls
11. **Risk Assessment (RA)** - Risk identification and assessment processes
12. **Security Assessment (CA)** - Security control assessment and testing
13. **System and Communications Protection (SC)** - System and network protection
14. **System and Information Integrity (SI)** - Information and system integrity

*Note: CMMC 2.0 consolidated the original 17 domains into 14 domains while maintaining all 110 controls. Asset Management, Recovery, and Supply Chain Risk Management controls are now distributed across the remaining domains.*

## üìä CMMC Maturity Levels

- **Focus**: Protect Federal Contract Information (FCI)
- **Controls**: 14 basic cybersecurity practices
- **Assessment**: Annual self-assessment

### Level 2: Intermediate Cyber Hygiene (Platform Focus)
- **Focus**: Protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)
- **Controls**: 110 security controls across 14 domains
- **Assessment**: Third-party assessment by C3PAO every 3 years
- **Processes**: Documented policies and procedures required

### Level 3: Good Cyber Hygiene
- **Focus**: Protection of Critical Program Information (CPI)
- **Controls**: Additional controls for advanced persistent threat protection
- **Assessment**: Third-party assessment by C3PAO
